Overview

Who is Yuta?

Yuta Okkotsu is one of the main characters in Jujutsu Kaisen. Born as a cursed human due to his childhood connection with Rika Orimoto, his love interest, Yuta initially struggles with controlling his immense cursed energy. With the guidance of Gojo Sensei, he grows into one of the most powerful sorcerers in the Jujutsu world.

Character Development

From a cursed outcry to a special grade sorcerer, Yuta's journey is one of redemption and self-discovery. He learns to harness his cursed power for good and becomes a reliable ally to his friends. His dedication to protecting others defines his character arc throughout the series.
